# 12-Month Startup Business Plan Prompt

A reusable prompt for generating a McKinsey / YC / a16z-grade 12-month execution roadmap for any business idea.

## How to use

1. Copy everything inside the `---` block below into a fresh chat.
2. Fill in the BUSINESS INFORMATION section with real answers.
3. Send. The output should be a real founder operating system, not generic advice.

---

I want you to act as an elite startup strategist, venture consultant, CFO, product strategist, branding expert, operations advisor, and growth marketer.

Create a highly detailed 12-month execution roadmap for my business idea.

The plan should feel like a real founder operating system and include:

timelines
budgets
financial projections
monthly goals
legal setup
product development
branding
marketing
launch strategy
scaling strategy
monetization
risk analysis
KPIs
staffing
investor readiness
trademark/IP guidance
operational systems

The output should be EXTREMELY detailed, practical, and realistic.
